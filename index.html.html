Privacy Policy

Track Your Profit  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

Summary: Track Your Profit stores all your data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No account registration is required.

1. Who We Are

Track Your Profit is an independent Android application developed by Alfonso Bergamini, based in Italy. You can contact us at: takeyourprofit.info@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

Track Your Profit does not collect any personal data. All information you enter in the app — including trade records, usernames, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (LocalStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial account information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other personal data

3. How Your Data Is Used

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use, process, or share any information about you. The data you enter in the app is used solely to display your trading history and statistics within the app on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, advertisers, analytics services, or any other external entities. There are no third-party SDKs or tracking libraries integrated into the app.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. You are in full control of your data at all times. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data from your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Track Your Profit is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors. If you believe a minor has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View your data directly within the app
  • Delete your data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app
  • Export your trade history using the built-in PDF export feature

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: takeyourprofit.info@gmail.com
